Overview
Fitness Mirror is a home fitness tool that helps people who want to go to the gym but can't persist in making exercise plans  tailored their needs.
Problem statement
How might we create a relaxing and efficient exercising experience to motivate people who wants to exercise?
Solution
01. Easy Onboarding

Fitmirror designed for your needs

By swiping images and introductions of Fitmirror, get excited of exercising.
02. Sign up/Log in

Provide basic information and get started

Fitmirror requires basic information like gender, date of birth, weight and height to get started. Don't forget to select your goal!
03. Exercise plan

Smart plan help you keep track of exercise

Fitmirror will generate a personalized plan for everyone. Enjoy both exercise plan and meal plan.
The context
Americans aren’t meeting national guideline for exercise

Reflection
Challenge
As we gathered so much information from users, it took us a while to do an in-depth synthesis. It was really helpful for me to keep documenting everything I learned. Journaling allowed me to periodically re-examine my observations, which helped me pull together new ideas and insights, and led the team to a clear path of the problem and move the project forward.
Next step
Due to time constraints, we did not conduct enough usability tests; I would like to create more iterations. In addition, I'd also like to develop other features such as meal plans and community features.
